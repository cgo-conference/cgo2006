Visa Letters

Those requiring visa letters for CGO must send an email to Christoph von Praun at praun AT us.ibm.com with a subject line "visa letter request".

Visa letters will only be issued to:

  • Speakers/Presenters
  • Committee Members
  • Attendees who have paid their registration fee in full and are not from one of the countries embargoed by the U.S. Treasury Departments Office of Foreign Assets Control.

Visa letters will state only the following facts:

  • Conference title, dates and location
  • That the requester has paid the required registration fee in full
  • If they are either a committee member, speaker, or presenter

If you have any question regarding the registration process, please contact the CGO-4 Registration Chair Christoph von Praun at (praun AT us.ibm.com).
