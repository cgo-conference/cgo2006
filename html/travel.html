CGO-4 Student Travel Support

A limited number of junior faculty and student travel grants are available to those attending CGO. Grants will be no less than $200, but no more than actual expenses. Applications will be accepted up to the Tuesday, March 28, 2006. Applicants that are early registered will receive preference. Actual payment will occur at the close of the conference. Preference will be given to conference presenters, then workshop presenters, then attendees.

To apply for a grant please send an email to Christoph von Praun (praun AT us.ibm.com) with the subject line "CGO Travel Grant". The following information in the message body:

  • Your name.
  • Your affiliation.
  • Whether you are a student or a junior faculty member.
  • Whether you are a co-author of a paper in the main symposium or one of the workshops. (Please give the name of the paper as well.)
  • Your estimated costs.
